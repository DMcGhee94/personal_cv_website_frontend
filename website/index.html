Inov8 Consulting Ltd

Lead Consultant

June 2016 - Present

I joined Inov8 as a Trainee Consultant immediately after gaining my University degree and immediately got put into training with SAS which set me on the path to become a data professional. After one week with Inov8 and a fast track training course in SAS with Newtyne, I started a contract with Tesco Bank in the Credit Risk department to manage their ETL batch processes as part of a BAU (Business as Usual) role.

Over the course of the next year, I attained both the base and advanced programmer certifications in SAS and was promoted from a trainee to a fully fledged consultant within Inov8 after completing my probation period.

In the years to follow, I have completed various training courses in different areas of data engineering, gained multiple certifications in different languages and tools, completed various projects for our clients including a migration from SAS 9.2 (On-prem) to SAS 9.4 (in cloud) and built a full ETL pipeline for Collections & Recoveries data including BI Reporting.

Within Inov8, I am in charge of running the systems we use to run the business including Microsoft Office, a third-party timesheet portal, Azure Active Directory, AWS, GCP and more. I also used tools like Microsoft Power Platform to automate some of the manual tasks that members of the business have to do manually, like sending out the payslips we receive from our accounting firm. Instead of sending them out via email manually, a Power Automate Flow will now detect the files arriving in the mailbox, process and load them into each employees private folder for documents like this and inform them that their slip is now available. Not only does this reduce the man hours required to complete tasks like this, but it also removes the manual element of it, reducing human error.

Having built my skills within Inov8 and proven my reliability, I have worked my way up through the business, being promoted to a Lead Consultant and now managing a team of between 4 and 6 people at a time. Not only that, but I am now closely involved in making decisions that affect the business and how it is run by providing my opinions on suggested changes or ways of moving forward.

Tesco Bank (Via Inov8 Consulting Ltd)

Regulatory Reporting Developer

October 2023 - Present

I was brought in to Tesco Bank specifically to work on an urgent regulatory request from the Financial Conduct Authority (FCA) regarding Credit Card customers in persistent debt.

This involed sourcing 200+ metrics on a sample of accounts for each of their statements for a period of roughly 7 years. This sampling was determined by account characteristics which was re-assessed for each month within the date period.

The request itself was segmented into 4 separate sub-reports, the first 2 of which have been successfully completed and supplied to the FCA, the remaining 2 are in progress currently.

This has been a fantastic contract for me to work on, allowing me to use my extensive Tesco Bank Credit Card's knowledge, SAS programming skills, data management best practices and stakeholder management capabilities. Not only that, due to the amount of time it takes to run 7 years worth of data, having to make the process as efficient as possible, and also simple to re-run and produce the same result was absolutely paramount to this projects success.
